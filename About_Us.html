ABOUT US

Formerly three independent branches (MHP – Curzon Avenue, MHP – Dean House and MHP – Green Street) Alma Healthcare Centre is an amalgamation of the three practice sites which relocated under one roof. MHP – Alma Healthcare Centre came into existence as a branch of Medicus Health Partners on the 14th of June 2021. Patients can now attend any site, clinics and get support wherever they see the Medicus Logo.

Support for COVID-19 has also been a focus, running the GP Assessment Centre at Chase Farm Hospital and opening one of the 3 Vaccine Hubs in Enfield at the Carlton House location has given the Partnership a wealth of learning to share with colleagues.
